About Happy Paws

At Happy Paws, professional groomers provide exceptionally premium quality services! You can be sure that your pet will receive an individual approach and will be offered the maximum of useful and enjoyable procedures depending on the breed and condition of the coat.

Today, thanks to grooming, the appearance and fur of our four-legged friends look like a real work of art, emphasizing the charm and individuality of each breed of cats and dogs.

Historical note about grooming

Few people know, but grooming originated in France. Louis XVI officially declared the miniature poodle a court breed. At that time, poodles were cut exclusively like lions, since they served their owners as hunting dogs.

To be closer to the aristocrats, townspeople and bourgeois also got dogs of the court breed and adopted the tradition of doing a popular haircut, as well as carefully caring for the pet’s coat.
